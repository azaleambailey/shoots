Photographer

Azalea Bailey

I'm a portrait photographer drawn to the quiet moments — the ones that happen between poses, between words. My work lives at the intersection of natural light and genuine emotion, capturing people as they truly are rather than who they think they should be in front of a lens. Based locally, shooting on a Nikon D3500 with an 18–70mm, I believe the best camera is the one that gets out of the way of the story.
